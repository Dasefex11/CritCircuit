# CritCircuit

CritCircuit is a mobile-first gaming news and streamer culture site focused on original articles, new game stories, and creator drama explainers.

## Launch Target

Planned launch date: **May 15, 2026**

## Current Pages

- `index.html` - homepage with the daily top 3 articles, latest news signals, favorite streamers, and watchlist.
- `articles.html` - article directory and social-ready captions.
- `articles/survival-games-2026.html` - long-form article.
- `articles/console-style-pc-gaming.html` - long-form article.
- `articles/streamer-drama-receipts.html` - long-form article.
- `styles.css` - full site styling and mobile layout.
- `ads.js` - hides empty ad slots until ad provider code is added.

## Current Editorial Plan

- Post **3 main articles per day**.
- Keep the homepage focused on the newest 3 articles.
- Move older articles into the article archive rather than deleting them.
- Use social captions to drive traffic back to the article pages.

## Launch Checklist

- Buy and connect `critcircuit.com`.
- Host the site on Netlify or another static host.
- Replace any placeholder URLs with the live domain if needed.
- Add privacy policy, about, and contact pages.
- Add analytics before launch.
- Add RSS feed before social automation.
- Test homepage and article pages on mobile.
- Verify article previews on Facebook, Discord, and X after the domain is live.

## Daily Codex Update Prompt

```text
Update CritCircuit with 3 fresh gaming/streamer articles for today. Make the homepage show the newest 3 articles first, move older articles into the archive/index, update social captions, and keep everything mobile-friendly.
```
